What is ?

collects and shows general data from several races. Nowadays running and other competitive sports are coming an important part of our lives. The number and frequency of races are increasing as well the number of participants. Different kind of people are starting to run and training, joining to races and they check their improvement with tracker applications. In addition, most of the races use chip devices and other trackers and they have a reliable way of measuring the data of each participant.

In we decided to analyze these data in order to extract useful and interesting data; plotting graphs and make this sport of running more interesting for the people who loves it.
