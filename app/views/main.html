What is ?

collects and shows general data from several races. Nowadays running and other competitive sports are becoming an important part of our lives. The number and frequency of races are increasing as well as the number of participants. Different kind of people are starting running and traininig, joining races and checking their improvement with tracking devices. In addition, most of the races use provide devices and other trackers and they have a reliable way of measuring the data of each participant.

In we decided to analyze these data in order to extract useful and interesting information; plotting graphs and making this sport of running more interesting for people who love it.

explores the idea of circuits. A circuit is formed by several races where there is a final classification for the whole circuit. How does work?

Crunching and serving the data

Receive data and plot the charts

The final application uses AngularJS and other technologies. It connects to the server to extract the info and finally plots the data in nice charts. Take a look and explore the web services!
