What is Runstats?

Runstats collects and show general data from some races. Nowadays running and sports live are coming an important of our live. The number of races are increasing as well the number of participants. Different kind of people are starting to run and training, joining to races and check their improvement with tracker applications. In addition, most of the races use chip devices and other trackers and they have a reliable way of measuring the data of each participant.

In Runstats we decided to analyze this data in order to check if we can extract useful and interesting data; plotting graphs and make this sport of running more interesting for the people who love it.
